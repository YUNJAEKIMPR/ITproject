Pineapple Upside-Down Cupcakes


ingredients



— pineapple

— warm butter

— gooey brown sugar

— cherries


description



1. cut each pineapple slice into four pieces; set aside.

2. in a large bowl, beat the cake mix, oil, eggs and reserved pineapple juice with an electric mixer on low speed for 30 seconds.

3. beat on medium speed for 2 minutes, scraping the bowl occasionally

4. in a small bowl, stir together melted butter and brown sugar.

5. spoon 1-½ teaspoons into each muffin cup

6. top each with two pineapple pieces. place a cherry half, cut side up, in the centre of the pineapple pieces

7. spoon ¼ cup batter into each cup

8. bake 20 to 25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the centre comes out clean. cool 5 minutes

9. run a knife around the edge of the cupcakes to loosen. invert the pan onto a cookie sheet and lift up

nutrition



calories 180

total fat 8g

protein 1g

total carbohydrate 25g

sugars 18g
